How I write clear technical English

In this subject, my goal is not to sound "fancy". My goal is to be clear, correct, and professional. This is the structure I follow when I write lab activities or project documentation.

My default structure

  1. Purpose: what we want to achieve.
  2. Context: lab setup, IPs, tools, constraints.
  3. Steps: numbered, short sentences, one action per step.
  4. Verification: commands that prove it works.
  5. Evidence: screenshots + outputs, linked in the portfolio.

Useful sentence starters

  • "The goal of this task is to..."
  • "First, we configure..."
  • "To verify the configuration, we run..."
  • "The expected output is..."
  • "If the service fails, we check logs using..."
